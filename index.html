Source Summit is a peer-to-peer learning environment. We connect doers from a range of backgrounds to find better solutions to our current challenges. .

We run short problem-solving sessions where someone presents one of their challenges to the group, or where someone shares recent successes in an open discussion.

The topics are set by you, the participants.

We start the day by setting the schedule together. The rest of the day is parallel tracks of short sessions. You'll have the choice of 12 to 18 interactive topics throughout the day.
