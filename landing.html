Source Summit: Humanitech

It's no longer necessary to choose between high-growth opportunities and social impact. When it comes to humanitech, these have converged.
When
Monday, September 12, 2016 from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (BST)
Where
London - Location TBC, London, EC1, United Kingdom

An unconference for technology that helps humanity

Source Summit: Humanitech is an opportunity to learn from teams that have deployed new business models and built distribution for water filtration, energy and sanitation products. You'll meet founders that have created new agiculture and mining tools by working alongside labourers. It's a chance to trade notes with STEM educators and capacity builders.
It's a way for you to help take much-needed technologies to the markets that need them most.

define: Humanitech

a technology product where the social impact is built into product use. eg. water filters, education, sanitation, food technology.

The convergence of global trends

Follow the high-growth markets around the world, or follow the challenges that attract the brightest and most ambitious engineers, or follow the groundswell of social enterprises - and you'll end up at the same place: humanitech.
The highest-growth markets in the world are rising out of poverty en masse, and their earning potential is driving commercial forces towards solving core infrastructure issues.
A curated event connecting Europe, Asia and Africa
On September 12th, we have an opportunity to connect the real doers in this space, between Africa and Europe. The Africa Priize For Engineering Innovation, one of Source Institute's projects run with The Royal Academy Of Engineering, is meeting in London. Africa Prize's alumni are joining us at Source Summit: Humanitech.
We're also connecting the community around Koobe Global (a design and manufacturing firm for startups from Foxconn family)- some of the best minds in hardware design and manufacturing.
This spans continents, and what better place than London for us all to converge?

Source Summit - a practical peer-to-peer learning day

* An open conference where you set the topics based on your challenges. Source Summit is more timely and relevant than traditional conference formats.
* Curated from a wide range of disciplines and geographies, connecting you with a whole range of new perspectives.
* Participants leave with actionable insight that they apply within a week or less.
