About Source

Source runs educational events where the learners set the topics based on their goals, and we bring in people with useful experience to share. 95% of our participants leave with actionable insight that they apply within a week or less, and most make long-lasting connections to collaborators and advisors.

Our events range from small dinners with facilitated skill sharing, through to large-scale, multi-track unconferences where everyone creates a schedule of hands-on learning sessions together.
